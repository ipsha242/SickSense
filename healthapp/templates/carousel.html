about us

we take care of your healthy life

Our website SickSense is an online platform that provides a disease prediction system along with doctor's appointments. We have combined healthcare and cutting-edge technology to create our prediction system. A user can easily log in or signup and then enter their symptoms, our website will diagnose them accordingly, they are then provided with doctor's contacts and can schedule an appointment with them.

SickSense aims to provide an accurate diagnosis of a disease. Our objective is to make healthcare more accessible, time-saving, and convenient. Our website also has a section for home remedies where a few safe and basic remedies are present for every disease. We have recruited expert doctors with immense experience in their domains to treat their patients accordingly. We are also constantly adding new features to improve our website and make it more robust. Our goal is to provide the best possible healthcare solutions and disease diagnosis online.
